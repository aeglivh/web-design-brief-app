# Branding — Quick Decision Doc

## What the product does
Multi-tenant SaaS for web designers: white-label client intake forms, AI-generated briefs, quotes, and contracts. Designers share a branded link, clients fill it out, everyone saves hours.

## Current name: Briefflow
- Pros: descriptive, available, says what it does
- Cons: generic SaaS-y, "flow" suffix is overused (Webflow, Airflow, Kissflow...)

## Alternative directions

### Descriptive (says what it does)
- **Briefsmith** — crafting briefs
- **Scopepad** — scoping projects
- **Briefbase** — where briefs live
- **Kickbrief** — kick off projects

### Short & punchy (memorable, brandable)
- **Brieff** — brief with a twist, easy domain
- **Skopi** — scope + i, friendly
- **Onbo** — onboarding, short
- **Takein** — intake, reversed

### Professional / studio-oriented
- **ClientPort** — portal for clients
- **StudioBrief** — speaks to the audience
- **DesignScope** — scoping for designers
- **BriefDesk** — your desk for briefs

### Abstract (no literal meaning, pure brand)
- **Cova** — short, premium feel
- **Narro** — narrowing scope
- **Parlo** — from "parlare" (to speak/discuss)
- **Volta** — a turn, a new project

## Decision criteria
1. **Domain available?** Check .com and .co
2. **Says enough** — can someone guess what it does?
3. **Short** — ideally under 8 chars
4. **Not cringe** — would a designer put this in their workflow?
5. **Looks good in a logo** — single word, clean typography

## What needs the name
- Login page (logo + wordmark)
- Browser tab / favicon
- Generated brief footer ("Powered by ___")
- Marketing site (future)
- Supabase project name (cosmetic, low priority)

## Action
Pick one, buy the domain, update 2-3 strings in code. Done in 10 minutes.
